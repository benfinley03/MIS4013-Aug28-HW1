Turtwig is the grass starter from the Pokemon Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum (DPP) games, which is Generation 4.

It evolves from Turtwig to Grotle (lvl. 18) to Torterra (lvl. 32). This makes it more unique than other starters, which traditionally evolve at levels 16 and 36.

Turtwig is my favorite Pokemon because it was my first ever Pokemon in my first game.
