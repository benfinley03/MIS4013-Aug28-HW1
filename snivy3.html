Snivy is the grass starter from the Generation 5 games, which are Pokemon Black and White and Black and White 2 (2 years after the events of Black and White).

The Snivy evolution line is pretty boring, design wise. It goes from Snivy to Servine (lvl. 17) to Serperior (lvl. 36).

Snivy and it's evolution line isn't too interesting. The Black and White games are pretty cool, though. The best part about Snivy is its later evolution's hidden ability, Contrary. It makes all stat changes have the oppotiste effect. This means the move Leaf Storm increases Special Attack by 2 stages instead of decresing by 2 stages. This makes it so you can do damange and set up at the same time to sweep the enemy team.
